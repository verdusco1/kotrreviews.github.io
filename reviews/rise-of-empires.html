Rise of Empires is one of those mobile games made to look like the other mobile games that look like this. Alliteration? Maybe. But you know exactly what I'm talking about when you look at it.

I came in only wanting to spend a few minutes a day and build a small castle, but now I'm a Rank 4 Guardian of an alliance and am pretty much 2nd in command.

My castle is humble because I need to make sure every base is of equal level before upgrading my castle. My army is weak and I am pummelled 3 times a day (how do they keep finding me?). My only friend doesn't respond to my messages and my alliance mates do not acknowledge my greatness, but I can't stop playing.

It's easy to do a few things every few hours and get tons of rewards to do more things. This isn't pay-to-play (I'm sure you could pay to win though), I only spent money to change my profile picture and I have come super far and still have much more to go before even thinking about paying for anything.

The dragon that you see on the art kind of sits around and kills bad guys that hang around your castle so don't mind him. The game is fantastic when your army goes to battle against the opposition! You can do this often and even completely destroy a base of operations for someone (suck it, my old alliance leader that never ranked me up).

You can level up your leaders, research more effective things and make armor, it isn't really innovative or new, it's just not evil and we know that the evil ones force paywalls everywhere.

To be honest, I think more online games need to take a page from this Realm of Empire's book. Make paying an option and a very tempting one, but don't make the game less fun for those who won't pay.

This game may be one of the ones that look like it-- I love that term, but it shines in the fact that you can do everything, including beat everyone, without spending a dime.

5/5 we need more games like this one out there.

Why would you buy Monopoly for your mobile device when you could purchase a perfectly reasonable version of the board game at your nearby Target/Walmart?

Well, for one, they never have the original. They only have weird cash grabs that go with new games or movies. How do we have that high of a demand for those?

Secondly, the one you can buy is like $30 and this one is like $2.99 and Sebastian won't cheat as a banker. I know you didn’t have that much money before Seba, I WILL CATCH YOU ROBBING THE BANK.

And three, this mobile app is actually better than the tabletop version. That’s right, Monopoly just needed technology.

Let's get the obvious stuff out of the way. There's no set up required, the computer is your bank, you don't need friends (the AI and Tom from MySpace will always be my friend, sniffle), and you can put it down and continue at any time. It’s Monopoly without the hassle.

I don't need to sell you on the concept of monopoly, it's been around longer than some of our grandparents. This version has no bugs, it's intuitive, it has multiple boards, and pieces to choose from (different boards and exclusive non-original pieces come at an extra price, understandably).

I can even play online so I don't need to share a mobile device. This is Monopoly with a luxury setting on! Go buy this! It's worth playing again!

5/5 Will play again.
